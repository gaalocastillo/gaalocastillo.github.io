I am a BSc. in Computer Science from Ecuador. Currently I am a Master of Science in Computer Science student, following the Artificial Intelligence track at Universite Paris-Saclay, France. I obtained my bachelor’s degree in Computer Science at Escuela Superior Politécnica del Litoral (ESPOL), located in Guayaquil, Ecuador.

I worked as a Data Scientist at Claro Ecuador (América Móvil group), the largest Ecuadorian telecommunications company, from 2019 to 2021. During my undergraduate studies and later (2017-2020) I worked as a Research Assistant at the Big Data Research Group of ESPOL under the guidance of Prof. Carmen Vaca. Also, in 2018 I participated as a Software Engineering Intern at the Google Summer of Code program and implemented a web application module for SPDX, a project of The Linux Foundation.
